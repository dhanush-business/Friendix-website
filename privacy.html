Privacy Policy

Friendix.ai values your privacy. We do not store personal data unless you explicitly enable the memory feature. When memory is enabled you will be prompted and can manage or delete stored items at any time.

What we collect

Only the data you provide in conversations and optionally saved memory. We don't collect payment or device-identifying data by default.

Third parties

We do not sell or share your personal conversations. If we use third-party services (hosting, analytics), only minimal metadata may be shared and is handled under contractual safeguards.
